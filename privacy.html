Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 1, 2024

At TextToolHub.world, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our website and services.

Information We Collect

TextToolHub is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal information to provide and improve our services:

Information You Provide Directly

  • Text Input: When you use our text tools (reverse text generator, small text maker, Morse code translator), the text you input is processed entirely in your browser. We do not store, save, or transmit this text to our servers.
  • Contact Information: If you contact us through our contact form, we collect your name and email address to respond to your inquiry.

Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage Data: We collect anonymous usage statistics to understand how our tools are used and to improve user experience. This includes information about which tools are most popular, typical session length, and general geographic location (country-level).
  • Technical Information: We may collect browser type, operating system, device type, and IP address (anonymized) for analytics and security purposes.

How We Use Your Information

We use the limited information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain our text manipulation tools
  • To improve and optimize our website and services
  • To respond to user inquiries and provide customer support
  • To analyze usage patterns and trends
  • To detect, prevent, and address technical issues or security vulnerabilities
  • To comply with legal obligations

Data Processing and Storage

One of TextToolHub's core privacy features is that all text processing happens locally in your browser:

  • Text you input into our tools (reverse text generator, small text maker, Morse code translator) is never sent to our servers
  • All text transformations occur entirely on your device using JavaScript
  • We do not have access to, nor do we store, the content you process through our tools
  • Any text you copy from our tools remains on your device

Third-Party Services

We use a limited number of third-party services to support our website:

  • Google Analytics: We use Google Analytics to understand how users interact with our website. This service collects anonymous usage data and helps us improve our services. Google Analytics may use cookies and similar technologies.
  • Advertising: We display advertisements through Google AdSense to support the free operation of our website. These ads may use cookies to display personalized content.
  • Hosting Services: Our website is hosted on standard web hosting platforms that process necessary technical data to serve our content.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

TextToolHub uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience:

  • Essential Cookies: Necessary for the basic functionality of our website
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how visitors interact with our website
  • Advertising Cookies: Used by our advertising partners to display relevant ads

You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings. However, disabling essential cookies may affect the functionality of our website.

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • All data transmission between your browser and our website uses HTTPS encryption
  • We regularly update our software and security protocols
  • We limit access to personal information to authorized personnel only
  • We conduct regular security assessments of our systems

While we strive to protect your information, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security but we work diligently to implement best practices.

Data Retention

We retain different types of information for different periods:

  • Text Input: Not retained - processed locally and immediately discarded
  • Analytics Data: Retained for up to 26 months in anonymized form
  • Contact Information: Retained for as long as necessary to address your inquiry and for a reasonable period thereafter for record-keeping purposes

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information:

  • Right to Access: You can request information about the personal data we hold about you
  • Right to Correction: You can request correction of inaccurate personal information
  • Right to Deletion: You can request deletion of your personal information
  • Right to Object: You can object to certain processing of your personal information
  • Right to Data Portability: You can request a copy of your personal data in a structured format

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information provided in the "Contact Us" section.

Children's Privacy

TextToolHub is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us, and we will take steps to delete such information.

International Data Transfers

TextToolHub is operated globally. Your information may be processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your information when transferred internationally.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:

  • Through our contact form
  • By email: privacy@texttoolhub.world

We will respond to your inquiry as soon as possible, typically within 2-3 business days.
